Who we are ?

Future Tech Information Technology has been originally founded in the year 2000 and re-established in the year 2006 by the registration number, No. 457 of 2006 – 2007, aiming to deliver ICT services throughout the nationwide in Myanmar. And throughout the years of experience in the market, it has developed a highly qualified and experienced staffs specializing in Consultation, Systems Designing, Customer Support, Sales and Marketing.

Future Tech is looking to the future with confident in the technology to help resolving the most sophisticated customers’ ICT requirements and satisfying their needs even more. Enjoying every moment in our work because we do what we love, and we love what we do.

To develop and deliver state of the art communication technology solutions; with very professional customer services and support, aiming at customer satisfaction and a commitment to market needs, supporting progressive organizations in the region and continue improving our ability in Consultation, System Design, Project Implementation, System Integration, Sales Support & Customer Care to enhance our customer’s satisfaction to make it a joyful and fruitful experience.

Future Tech also aims to respond to each customer’s requirements with an optimized solution, not only to satisfy these specific requirements, but also, to exceed the customer’s expectations.
